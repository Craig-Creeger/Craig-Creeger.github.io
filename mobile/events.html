Mobile Events and Gestures

Events

Touch-based devices like cell phones and tablets, do not have a mouse, and therefore do not understand the click, mouseMove, or other mouse events. However, those devices will automatically convert a click event to a touch event after something like 300ms.

There are Javascript libraries that will help to abstract away these differences.
