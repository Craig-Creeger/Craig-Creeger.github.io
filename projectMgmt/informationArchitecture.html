Information Architecture

Definition

According to Wikipedia, Information architecture (IA) is the art and science of organizing and labelling websites, intranets, online communities and software to support usability.

From a practical standpoint, the purpose of IA is to define and document the

  • Goals and Purpose
  • User Experience and Audience
  • Site Content and Structure/navigation
  • Visual Design

of a website.

Webmonkey has a good article on IA. For your convenience, I have created a PDF document of that same article.

Execution and Deliverables

To define the four bulleted items above, you can follow the instructions in the Webmonkey article.
