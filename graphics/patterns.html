Patterns

Definition

A pattern is a small, repeating graphic used as a background. For example, the background in the following block is made up of this image:

You’ll notice that the pattern image is very small at only 28px by 28px and 224 bytes. It also blends together seamlessly when repeated. As a bonus, this particular pattern has a horrible optical illusion to it. ;-)

Placing a pattern on your background can be done similar to this:

<style type="text/css">
section {
	background: url(patternMesh.gif) repeat;
}
</style>

The Pattern8 website has a bunch of examples.

You can also make your own repeating pattern.
